FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About
Our Services

How long does the eligibility check take?

Our initial eligibility check is processed within 24-48 hours. Once you submit your information, one of our consultants will contact you to discuss your options based on your profile.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, your initial consultation is completely free of charge. We believe in providing value first and helping you understand your options before any commitments are made.

Which countries can I apply to?

We assist with applications to numerous countries including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, and many more. Our consultants will help identify the best destinations based on your academic background and career goals.

Do I need IELTS?

Requirements vary by institution and country. Many universities require English proficiency tests like IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE, but some offer waivers based on your academic background. Our experts will guide you on the specific requirements for your chosen destination.
